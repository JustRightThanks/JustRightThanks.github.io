Welcome to Standing Strong!

What is standing?

At it simplest, standing is honoring your marriage vows, despite what your spouse is doing.

Why stand?

  • to honor God

  • you want to be faithful

  • you love your spouse

  • for the welfare of your children

  • hope of reconciliation
